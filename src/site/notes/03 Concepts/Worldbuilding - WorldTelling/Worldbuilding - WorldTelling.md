# WorldTelling

## My working definition

Worldtelling is the practice of making a speculative world experienceable across different media, systems, and modes of participation.

It is related to worldbuilding, but it is not the same thing. Worldbuilding creates the world. Worldtelling is how the world is revealed, inhabited, navigated, played, read, watched, and interpreted.

## Why it matters to the Capstone

This may become the core term for the project. The Capstone is not only about telling a story. It is about designing a framework that lets audiences encounter a world at different levels of agency and participation.
