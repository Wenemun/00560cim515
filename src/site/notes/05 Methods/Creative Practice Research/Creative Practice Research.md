# Creative Practice Research

## What it is

Creative Practice Research treats creative making as both the method and the outcome of research.

## How I will use it

The creative work will act as a testbed for the framework. I will document how design choices are made, how the framework changes through making, and what the creative work reveals about multimodal worldtelling.

## Risks

- Scope creep
- Making too much and analysing too little
- Treating the world itself as the whole project
- Not capturing enough process evidence
