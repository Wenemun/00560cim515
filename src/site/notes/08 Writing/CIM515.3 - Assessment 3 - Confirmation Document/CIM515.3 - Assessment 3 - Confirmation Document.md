**Title: Confirmation Document**

|   |   |
|---|---|
|**Due**|Week 12|
|**Weighting**|40%|
|**Submission Format**|Document file submission|

**Brief**

The Capstone Project enables you to demonstrate the full range of your creative and academic skills and abilities. It provides a structured opportunity to display skill and innovation through the completion of a major creative work informed by substantial scholarly research.

The Confirmation Document is a scholarly document that communicates the design of your project, including the research question, literature review and methodology, and a timeline for the work still to be completed. 

Where a project requires ethics approval, a completed ethics application containing all of the requirements below may be submitted as your Confirmation Document. This should be a copy of the application that you submit to the LREC or HREA.

**Requirements**

The development of your confirmation document will be guided by your supervisor(s) and each section of the document will be covered during group webinars. Your project should be consistent with the project that you pitched in week 6, with only minor variations acceptable. 

The precise content of your milestone document may vary depending on your project, but ordinarily will contain the following elements:

- Title page
    
- Table of contents
    
- In introduction that includes any relevant context (social, cultural, political, and your own background relevant to the project), the research question (or area of investigation), and a description of the proposed creative work and its relationship to the exegesis
    
- A literature review (critical theoretical framing of the creative work)
    
- Methodology (what you will do to answer your research question)
    
- Timeline for completion (including consideration of the creative and scholarly components of your project)
    
- Bibliography (that can include all relevant literature, including literature you have yet to engage with)
