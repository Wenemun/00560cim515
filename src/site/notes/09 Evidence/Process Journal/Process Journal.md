# Process Journal

About: 

I am Seth Callaghan, a digital producer, UX designer, writer, visual artist and worldbuilder exploring how speculative worlds can become interactive, participatory digital experiences. 

This process journal tracks the messy development of my Capstone project: the research, decisions, doubts, design work, worldbuilding, prototyping and reflection that happen along the way.  It documents how the project changes over time, how the framework and creative output develop together, and how my thinking connects back to theory, methodology and practice. 

My Capstone project explores how speculative worlds can be designed as coherent, participatory storytelling systems across roleplaying, digital interaction and transmedia forms.
