## Questions

- Does this project involve human participants?
- Will anyone be interviewed, surveyed, observed, tested, or involved as a creative collaborator?
- Will playtesters be used?
- What data will be collected?
- How will data be stored, protected, retained, and destroyed?
- Are there power relationships, conflicts of interest, or participant risks?
- How will AI use be acknowledged?

## Notes

Even creative practice research can trigger ethics requirements if people are involved as participants, testers, crew, collaborators, or sources of data.
