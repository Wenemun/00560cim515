# MOC - Immutable Aeon Testbed

## Purpose

Immutable Aeon is the creative testbed for the Capstone framework.

It should demonstrate how a speculative world can be experienced across media, rather than simply described in a linear story.

## Testbed Questions

- What part of the world is best told through text?
- What part is best discovered through interaction?
- What part is best shown through visual design?
- What part is best experienced through play?
- What remains meaningful for someone who only wants to consume passively?
- What changes for someone who wants to actively participate?
